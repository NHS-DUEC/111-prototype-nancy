Parts of the body

Head, hair and face

Hair falling out or thinning Hair loss from any part of the body. Losing hair in patches or clumps. Head, face or neck injury with a cut or wound An injury to any part of the face (but not eyes or teeth), head or neck, which has cut the skin, within the last 7 days. Head, face or neck injury without a cut or wound An injury to the head, face or neck within the last 7 days that didn't cut the skin. Swollen or painful face or neck Swelling, lumps or pain in the face or neck. Swollen glands. Headache or migraine Throbbing or intense pain or a tight band around the head. Visual problems like flashing lights with a headache. Sinusitis A problem with the sinuses. Face pain around the cheeks, eyes or forehead. A runny nose and a high temperature.

Ears

Earache Ear pain, swelling, or fluid leaking from the ear. A painful blocked ear. New deafness with pain. Ringing in the ear. Hearing problems or blocked ear Hearing problems or deafness without earache. Blocked ear or fluid leaking from the ear with no pain. Fluid or wax coming from the ear with no loss of hearing Fluid, ear wax or watery discharge from one or both ears but without any loss of hearing. Something in the ear An object stuck inside the ear. An insect in the ear.

Eyes

Something in the eye Dirt, dust or an object has gone into the eyes. Something sprayed or splashed into the eyes. Eye injury or cut An injury to the eye or eye socket within the last 7 days that has caused a cut or wound. Eye injury without cut or wound An injury to the eye or eye socket within the last 7 days without any cut or wound. Eye or eyelid problems Sore, yellow, red or bloodshot eyes. Loss of or changes in vision. Swollen, sticky, itchy or sore eyelids. A lump on the eyelid.

Nose

Blocked or stuffy nose A congested or blocked nose. Sinus problems. Nosebleed A bleeding nose. Nosebleed after an injury A bleeding nose following an injury. Something stuck in the nose An object stuck inside a nostril.

Jaw, mouth and teeth

Mouth ulcers Mouth, lip, tongue or gum ulcers. Sore white or grey patches inside the mouth. Dental injuries Dental problems caused by an injury, such as knocked out or broken teeth. Injuries to the gums or the inside of the mouth. Toothache after an injury Toothache and pain caused by an injury, such as broken, cracked or knocked out teeth. Bleeding or injuries to the gums or mouth. Swelling or lumps on the face or jaw. Problems opening or closing the mouth. Toothache, swelling and other dental problems Bleeding from the mouth, teeth or gums. Painful teeth or gums. Swelling or lumps on the gums, mouth or jaw. A crown, bridge, fillings, denture or brace problem. A broken tooth, or teeth not caused by an injury. Locked or aching jaw Problems opening or closing the mouth or jaw.

Neck and throat

Sore throat A painful throat, with or without problems swallowing. A hoarse, croaky or lost voice, with or without a sore throat. Difficulty swallowing Having trouble swallowing or unable to swallow, without a sore throat. Swollen or painful face or neck Swelling, lumps or pain in the face or neck. Swollen glands.

Shoulders and arms

Shoulder problems Ache or stiffness in one or both shoulders. Unusual feelings or weakness. Swollen or painful arm Pain or swelling in the arm or elbow. Arm injury with a cut or wound An injury to the arm or shoulder in the last 7 days, that has caused a cut or wound. Arm injury without a cut or wound An injury to the arm, wrist or shoulder within the last 7 days, that has not cut the skin. A dislocated shoulder or arm. Cold arm or arm changing colour A cold, painless arm. White, blue, or black colour in one or both arms.

Wrists, hands and fingers

Hand or wrist injury with a cut or wound An injury to the hand or wrist within the last 7 days that has cut the skin. Hand or wrist injury without a cut or wound A hand or wrist injury in the last 7 days that hasn't cut the skin. Swollen or painful hand, finger or wrist Pain or swelling in the hand, fingers, nails or wrist. Finger or thumb injury with a cut or wound A finger or thumb injury within the last 7 days that has cut the skin. Finger or thumb injury without a cut or wound A finger or thumb injury within the last 7 days that hasn't cut the skin. Fingernail injury Any injury to the fingernail or nails in the last 7 days.

Chest and upper back

Chest or upper back injury with a cut or wound An injury to the chest or upper back (the collarbone to the bottom of the ribcage) within the last 7 days that has cut the skin. Chest or upper back injury without a cut or wound An injury to the chest or upper back within the last 7 days, that hasn't cut the skin. Injuries from the collarbone to bottom of the ribcage. Chest pain or breathing problems Any chest or upper back pain. Chest pain with or without arm, shoulder, neck or jaw pain. Tightness in the chest. Wheezing, panting or feeling out of breath. Difficult or noisy breathing. Breast problems Problems with the breast or nipple. Breast pain or lumps. Discharge or bleeding. Nipple or breast changes. Sore, tender breasts. Mastitis. Breastfeeding problems Problems getting the baby to attach to the breast. Cracked or sore nipples, or a painful breast that is making breastfeeding difficult.

Stomach and sides

Injury to the stomach or side of body with a cut or wound An injury to the tummy (abdomen), or sides (flank) within the last 7 days that has cut the skin. Stomach or side injury without a cut or wound An injury to the tummy (abdomen), or sides (flank) within the last 7 days that has not cut the skin. Stomach pain Tummy (abdominal) pain with or without other symptoms such as diarrhoea, vomiting or a high temperature. Pain in the side of the body Pain on either side of the body, between the ribs and hips. Affecting one side only at the back (love handles) or with stomach pain.

Lower back

Lower back injury with a cut or wound An injury to the lower back (below the rib cage) or bottom in the last 7 days with a cut, wound or graze. Lower back injury without a cut or wound An injury to the lower back (below the rib cage) or bottom in the last 7 days that hasn't cut the skin. Lower back pain Pain or ache in the lower part of the back.

Groin and genitals

Groin pain or swelling Pain or swelling in the groin (between the waist and upper thighs). Genital injury with cut or wound An injury to the genitals within the last 7 days that has cut the skin. Cuts, wounds or grazes anywhere on the vagina or the area between the anus and vagina. Genital injury without a cut or wound An injury to the penis, scrotum, testicles or between the anus and scrotum, within the last 7 days, that has not cut the skin. Itchy vagina Sore or itchy vagina. Late or missed period Late or missed periods. No periods. Periods haven't started. Periods have stopped. Bleeding from the vagina Heavy periods, bleeding after sex or unexpected bleeding. Bleeding or spotting when pregnant. Discharge from the vagina Vaginal discharge, with or without tummy pain. Vaginal lump or swelling Any lump, cyst, swelling or pain in or around the vagina. Swollen or sore labia. Something stuck in the vagina An item such as a tampon, cap, condom or other object, that's got stuck in the vagina. Penis, testicle or scrotum problems Male genital problems, with or without tummy pain. Something stuck in or around the penis An object stuck inside or around the penis.

Bottom

Something stuck in the bottom An object stuck in the back passage. Bottom pain, swelling lump or itch Pain, itching or swelling in or around the bum (anus or rectum). Lumps in or around the bottom. Bleeding from the bottom Spots of blood on the toilet paper. Streaks of blood in poo. Red or black coloured poo.

Hips, legs and feet

Leg injury with a cut or wound An injury to the leg, hip, thigh, knee, calf, shin or ankle in the last 7 days, that has a cut, wound or graze. Leg injury without a cut or wound A leg injury above the ankle, within the last 7 days, that didn't cut the skin. Swollen or painful legs, knees, ankles or feet Painful or swollen legs, knees, ankles or feet. Pain or swelling in the hips or bum. Cold leg or leg changing colour A cold, painless leg. White, blue or black colour in one or both legs. Ankle or foot injury with a cut or wound An injury to the ankle or foot that has any type of cut or wound. Ankle or foot injury without a cut or wound An injury to the ankle or foot that doesn't have any type of cut or wound. Sprained ankle. Toe injury with a cut or wound An injury to one or more toes within the last 7 days that has cut the skin. Toe injury without a cut or wound A toe injury within the last 7 days that hasn't cut the skin. Toenail injury An injury to one or more toenails within the last 7 days. Painful, broken, ingrown or loose toenails.
