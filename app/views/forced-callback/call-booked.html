What if I don’t get a call in 30 minutes?

It’s safe to wait a bit longer as this is not an emergency. If you’re worried call 999. This doesn’t mean an ambulance will be sent straight away - but they’ll tell you what to do next.

What if I miss the call?

A nurse will try and ring you up to 3 times. If they can’t speak to you they may send emergency services to make sure you’re ok.

Call 999.
