How 111 online works

  1. download a free app
  2. answer questions about your symptoms
  3. get advice on what NHS service you might need and how urgently

Call 111 if you're asking about:

  • someone under 17
  • dental services
  • mental health care

This app is run by Babylon Health who provide 111 online in your area. It’s free to use and points you to the right NHS care.

  • NHS 111

  • Provided by Babylon Health

If you don’t want to use an app call 111 for health advice.
