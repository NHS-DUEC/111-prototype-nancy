How does 111 work online?

Tell us the symptom you're concerned about and:

  1. answer a number of questions
  2. get advice on who you need to see, and how urgently
  3. find the right local NHS service for your needs

The service doesn't diagnose specific conditions.

This online service is for people aged 5 and over. It's not a substitute for a medical consultation. See terms and conditions.

If you're looking for NHS opening times or contact information, use NHS Choices.
