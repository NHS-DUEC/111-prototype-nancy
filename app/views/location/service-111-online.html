How 111 online works

This service is for people aged 5 and over.

You can:

  • answer questions about your symptoms - some are to rule out more serious conditions
  • get advice on what NHS service you might need and how urgently

The service is not a replacement for seeing a health professional. It doesn’t diagnose conditions.
