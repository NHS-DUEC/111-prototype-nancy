Thanks for agreeing to take our survey

Before you start, there are a few things you need to know:

If you need medical help call 111

We don’t monitor the survey every day and won’t see comments you leave about your symptoms or any requests for medical help.

You’re anonymous unless you choose not to be

The survey works by remembering the answers you gave to our questions and asking you about what happened next. Everything you say is confidential.

If you agree to do a follow up survey we’ll ask for your email. We’ll then be able to link your answers to your email address.

Your data is the NHS’s responsibility

Our survey is hosted by a company called Qualtrics, but we look after your data. Our privacy policy explains how. If you’ve given us your email, we can delete all your survey responses if you ask us to.
