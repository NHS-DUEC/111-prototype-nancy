You need to isolate yourself and stay indoors

If you’re on public transport, go home by the most direct route. Stay at least 2 metres away from people if you can.

If you’re away from home:

  • find a room where you can close the door
  • avoid touching people, surfaces and objects

To protect yourself and others, do not go to a GP, pharmacy or hospital

You should keep yourself separate from others in your household (self-isolate) until you have been spoken to 111. Read more details about self-isolation.

You need to get a coronavirus test

We need to ask you for some details so that we can arrange a test. But as your symptoms are mild you can look after yourself at home as you would do with normal flu.

To manage your symptoms while you wait you can:

  • take paracetamol
  • drink plenty of water
  • get lots of rest

You should be contacted within 24 hours.
