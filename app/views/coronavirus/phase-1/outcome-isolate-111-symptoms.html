You need to isolate yourself and stay indoors

If you’re away from home, find a room where you can close the door and wait by yourself until you have spoken to 111.

If you’re already on public transport, go home by the most direct route.

Read guidance on how to keep yourself and others safe (opens in a new tab or window), when self-isolating.

Call NHS 111. Do not go to your GP or a hospital.

Tell 111 that you are calling about coronavirus (COVID-19) symptoms.

You must tell them where you have travelled or about any contact you’ve had with someone with symptoms.

If you hear a recorded message, make sure you select the option to speak to someone.

It’s important to follow these steps to stop any possible spread of infection.
