Have you been in close contact with someone who has been told they have coronavirus (COVID-19)?

Close contact means:

  • living in the same house
  • contact with their body fluids
  • talking closely face-to-face for more than a few minutes
  • being coughed on
  • being within 2 metres of the person for more than 15 minutes
