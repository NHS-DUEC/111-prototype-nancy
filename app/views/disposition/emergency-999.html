Phone 999 now for an ambulance

The 999 operator will ask:

  • where help is needed — the area or postcode (or what’s nearby if you don’t know)
  • what phone number they can call you back on if you get cut off
  • what’s happened — tell them that 111 online told you to call

You’ll be asked more questions about your symptoms to check if you do need an ambulance.

If you don’t need an ambulance you’ll be given advice on what to do next.
