What happens when you get there

The pharmacist or nurse will decide if it’s safe to give you the medicine. This will be based on the information they have and questions they will ask you.

Information they need

To help find your NHS number and your summary care record you’ll be asked for your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • postcode

If you agree they can view your summary care record it should show the medicines you’ve been prescribed before.

If you don’t have an NHS number because you’re visiting the UK, take a list of all your medicines and the dose if you know that. Or if you have any old boxes take those with you.

Paying for medicine

You might have to pay for your medicine and prices can vary. Some pharmacists only charge standard NHS prices.
