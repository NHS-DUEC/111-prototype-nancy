Services near you that can help

The pharmacist or nurse will make a decision about whether it is safe to give you the medicine. This will be based on the information they have and their own assessment.

To help to find your NHS number and your summary care record you’ll be asked for your:

  • name
  • age
  • postcode

If you give permission to view your summary care record it should show any medicines you’ve been prescribed before.

If you don’t have an NHS number because you are visiting you might still get help. Take a list of all the medicines you take and the dose if you know that. Or if you have any old boxes take those with you.

You might have to pay for your medicine and prices can vary. Some pharmacists only charge standard NHS prices.
