Services near you that can help

The pharmacist or clinician will make a decision about whether it is safe to supply you with the medicine. This will be based on the information they have and their own assessment.

You’ll be asked for your name, age and postcode to help to find your NHS number and your summary care record. If you give permission to view your summary care record it should show any medicines you’ve been prescribed before.

You might have to pay for your medicine and prices can vary. Some pharmacists only charge standard NHS prices.

It’s helpful if you have a list of all the medicines you take, and the dose, if you know that. Or gather old boxes if you have any. If you don’t know all the details of what you take that’s ok.
