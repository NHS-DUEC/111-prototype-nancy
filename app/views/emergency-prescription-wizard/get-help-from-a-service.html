We’ve found services that can help

A pharmacist or nurse will ask you some questions. They’ll:

  • check your records for what you’ve been prescribed before
  • decide if it’s safe to give you the medicine

It’s helpful if you can take a list of all your medicines and the doses with you if you know that.

Emergency prescriptions can be more expensive

You might have to pay for your medicine even if you don’t normally. Prices can vary and NHS pre-payment cards may not be accepted.

Some pharmacists are working with the NHS to only charge standard NHS prices.
