A pharmacist or nurse will ask you some questions. They’ll:

  • check your records for what you’ve been prescribed before
  • decide if it’s safe to give you the medicine

It’s helpful if you can take a list of all your medicines and the doses with you if you know that.

Paying for medicine

You might have to pay for your medicine and prices can vary. Some pharmacists only charge standard NHS prices.
