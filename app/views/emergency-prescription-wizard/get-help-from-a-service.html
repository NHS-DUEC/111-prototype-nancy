What happens when you get there

The pharmacist or nurse will decide if it’s safe to give you the medicine, based on their own assessment.

Information they need

To help find your NHS number and your summary care record you’ll be asked for your:

  • name
  • date of birth
  • postcode

Your summary care record it should show any medicines you’ve been prescribed before. The pharmacist will ask for your permission to view it.

If you don’t have an NHS number take a list of all your medicines and the dose if you know that. Or if you have any old boxes take those with you.

Paying for medicine

You might have to pay for your medicine and prices can vary. Some pharmacists only charge standard NHS prices.
