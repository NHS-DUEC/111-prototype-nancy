Emergency prescriptions

You don’t always need a doctor to get an emergency supply of medicine you’re prescribed regularly.

A pharmacist can decide to give you a limited amount of medicine until you can get a repeat prescription.

Use this service if you’ve completely run out. If you’ve got enough to last until your GP or usual pharmacy is open you should contact them for a repeat prescription instead.

If you need medicine and you’re also feeling unwell, call 111 and speak to an adviser.
