Controlled drugs

Some prescription medicines are called ‘controlled drugs’ because they can only be given to you by a doctor. You can’t get any of this medicine from a pharmacy without a doctor’s permission.

Controlled medicines are most often used to treat pain.

They’re medicines like:

  • morphine
  • pethidine
  • methadone
  • temazepam
  • diazepam
  • codeine

Some hormones and steroids are controlled.
