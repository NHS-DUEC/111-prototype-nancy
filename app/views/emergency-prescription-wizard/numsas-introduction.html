Why we're asking for your details

Sending your details helps the pharmacist find your records in advance.

If they’re expecting you, standard NHS charges will apply. You won’t pay at all if you’re exempt from prescription charges.

We’ll ask for your phone number, name and home postcode.

Contacting them before you go

If you give the pharmacy a call they’ll tell you:

  • if they have your medicine in stock
  • when to collect it
  • what to do if they can’t help

If you don’t contact or visit the pharmacy they will have to call you to make sure you’re ok.
