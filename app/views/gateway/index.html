How 111 online works

The online service is the same as the 111 telephone service.

  1. You answer questions about your symptoms - some are to rule out more serious conditions
  2. You won’t get a diagnosis, but you’ll find out what to do next - what NHS service you might need and how urgently

Get help wherever you are

You might not always be at home when you need help. Tell us where you are to get the 111 online service in that area.
